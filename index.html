PROJECT ALIAS

Back in 2015 I did a one year course in Game Design, which ended opening my doors to worlds I didn't explore until then like programing, animation and the actual design. The course project was to do a short demo of a game created by our group.
Although it didn't got past this theory of a game, I always loved it and cherish this project, maybe 'cause it was the first one of this kind I ever did or because I think it had an amazing story.
I worked mainly as a environment artist and later on, when I attended University in Animation and did the trailler you can see here, animating, editing, designing the UI and dialogues by myself. This trailler shows what in my head this game could have been.

3D Modeling

When I was 19 I found 3D Modeling while I was pursuing a path for Concept Art in a Digital Art School in Lisbon called OddSchool. Since then I was very fascinated with modeling more than digital painting and I started doing stuff in my free time and learning online. You can see here some of my projects and my last showreel done. Most my works and done mainly with Zbrush and Maya.
